## Description (4000 chars max)

NetMonitor puts a full suite of professional network diagnostics in your pocket. Purpose-built for IT professionals, network engineers, and power users who need fast answers about their network — no subscriptions, no ads, no data collection.

**Dashboard**
See your entire network posture at a glance. Real-time connection status, WiFi details (SSID, BSSID, channel, security), gateway latency, and ISP information — all updating live.

**Network Map**
Discover every device on your local network with concurrent TCP probing. See hostnames, IP addresses, MAC addresses, and device types. Identify unknown devices instantly.

**10 Professional Network Tools**

• Ping — TCP-based latency testing with live graphs, min/avg/max statistics, and continuous streaming results.

• Port Scanner — Fast TCP connect scanning with common port identification. Know exactly what services are exposed on any host.

• DNS Lookup — Query A, AAAA, MX, TXT, CNAME, NS, SOA, and PTR records against any DNS server.

• Traceroute — Visualize the hop-by-hop path to any destination with per-hop latency measurements.

• Speed Test — Measure your download and upload bandwidth with a clean, minimal interface.

• WHOIS Lookup — Query domain registration data including registrar, nameservers, and expiration dates.

• Wake on LAN — Send magic packets to wake machines on your network. Save targets for one-tap access.

• Bonjour Discovery — Browse mDNS services on your local network. See every advertised service and its details.

• Web Browser — Built-in browser for accessing router admin panels and network device interfaces without leaving the app.

**Built Right**
• Native iOS 18+ with SwiftUI — fast, fluid, and designed for the platform.
• Swift 6 strict concurrency — rock-solid reliability, no race conditions.
• 100% on-device — all data stays on your iPhone using SwiftData. Zero analytics. Zero tracking.
• No account required. No subscription. No ads.

**Designed for Professionals**
NetMonitor uses a modern glass-card interface that presents dense network data clearly. Every tool streams results in real time so you see answers the moment they arrive, not after a loading spinner.

Whether you're troubleshooting a client's WiFi, auditing open ports on a server, or just want to know what's on your home network — NetMonitor is the tool that respects your time, your privacy, and your intelligence.
